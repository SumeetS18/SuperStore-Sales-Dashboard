# 📊 Online Sales Dashboard Analysis

## 🧭 Project Objective
The primary objective of this project is to contribute to business success by leveraging data analysis techniques—especially time series analysis—to uncover valuable insights and generate accurate sales forecasts. The project revolves around building an interactive dashboard to track and analyze online sales, helping stakeholders make informed decisions.

---

## ❓ Key Business Questions / KPIs
1. What is the total number of sales orders and items sold?  
2. How do sales and profits vary across different segments (e.g., Home Office, Cards, Online)?  
3. What is the average delivery time for orders?  
4. What are the monthly trends in sales and profit across years (YoY)?  
5. Which states generate the most sales and profit?  
6. What is the sales distribution by shipping mode?  
7. Can we forecast the sales for the next 15 days based on historical data?

---

## 🔄 Project Process

### 1. Data Preparation
- Imported sales data from online retail platforms.
- Cleaned and transformed data to remove inconsistencies.
- Created relationships and joins between relevant tables.

### 2. Dashboard Creation
- Identified key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Designed an intuitive and visually appealing dashboard in Power BI / Tableau.
- Implemented various charts: bar, pie, donut, clustered bar, scatter, line, area, map, and slicers.

### 3. Analysis & Insights
- Used customized visualizations to explore trends in sales, profit, and segment performance.
- Incorporated drill-down features, filters, and slicers to allow users deeper interaction with data.

### 4. Forecasting
- Applied time series forecasting models on historical sales data.
- Predicted the next 15 days of sales to aid in planning and decision-making.

---

## 🔍 Project Insights
- **Sales Distribution:** Home Office contributed nearly 48% of sales, showing a significant customer base in that segment.  
- **Profit Margins:** Despite good sales volume, profit wasn't uniformly distributed—indicating cost or pricing inefficiencies.  
- **YoY Trends:** There was a noticeable spike in both sales and profit in 2020 compared to 2019.  
- **State Analysis:** Certain states consistently performed better, suggesting the need for focused marketing or logistics expansion there.  
- **Delivery Metrics:** The average delivery duration was approximately 4 days, which is a critical KPI for logistics optimization.

---

## ✅ Final Conclusion
This dashboard project showcases the power of data visualization and forecasting in understanding and optimizing online sales. With effective use of time series analysis, interactive visuals, and KPI tracking, the business gains actionable insights that support strategic growth, improved customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.
